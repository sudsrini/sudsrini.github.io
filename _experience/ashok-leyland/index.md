## Project 1: BS4 to BS6 Process Alignment

## Objective
Update Work Instruction Sheets and Standard Operating Procedures for BS4 engines to meet BS6 emission standards, improving clarity and efficiency in engine assembly processes.

## Process
- Reviewed existing BS4 engine SOPs and identified necessary modifications for BS6 compliance
- Updated 20+ engine assembly processes to incorporate new standards and reduce ambiguity
- Collaborated with engineers and shop floor personnel to validate changes for real-world application

## Result
- Delivered finalized BS6-compliant SOPs and WIS documentation
- Improved clarity in assembly instructions, reducing errors and supporting smoother adoption of BS6 standards

## Project 2: Antilock Braking System (ABS) Sensor Issue Resolution

## Objective
Improve the straight-pass rate of vehicles in Road Tests by reducing ABS failures caused by faulty sensors.

## Process
- Analyzed ABS test data from 150+ vehicles
- Used Pareto analysis to identify failure causes -- found 70% were linked to faulty sensors detected too late in the process
- Proposed and implemented early-stage sensor testing at the Axle Shop using a market-available ABS sensor tester

## Result
- Reduced late-stage ABS failures by shifting detection earlier in the assembly process
- Improved straight-pass percentages in Road Tests and reduced rework
