## Objective
Identify and eliminate inefficiencies in the drop-arm painting process to improve throughput and cycle efficiency.

## Process
- Performed a detailed time study to measure cycle times and map bottlenecks across the painting workflow
- Identified non-value-adding activities reducing throughput
- Designed a specialized drying trolley in SolidWorks to streamline workflow and eliminate the primary bottleneck

## Results
- Increased process efficiency from 2 to 3 cycles per hour
- Boosted maximum output from 64 to 152 units per hour
- Eliminated key inefficiencies, creating a more reliable and repeatable production line
