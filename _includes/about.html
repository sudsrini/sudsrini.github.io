About Me

I'm Sudarsan Srinivasan, a Product Design Engineer with 4+ years of industry experience and a Master's in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Washington.

My background spans automotive powertrain design at Bajaj Auto, systems integration at The Boring Company, and consumer hardware NPD at SharkNinja. The common thread isn't the industry. It's the kind of work. I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of disciplines, where a good solution requires understanding mechanics, manufacturing, testing, and the people involved all at once.

I specialize in taking designs from concept through validation, with a strong foundation in DFM/DFA, GD&T, tolerance analysis, and FEA. I'm now focused on consumer hardware and electronics, where that end-to-end design thinking has the most direct impact on the people using the product.
