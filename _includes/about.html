About Me

Hi! I'm Sudarsan Srinivasan, a Mechanical & Product Design Engineer with 3.5+ years of experience designing and validating high-volume mechanical components in the automotive industry, currently pursuing a Master's in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington.

I specialize in mechanical product design, including CAD, DFM/DFA, GD&T, tolerance analysis, and FEA, with a strong focus on manufacturability, reliability, and production readiness. My work spans aluminum castings, injection-molded plastic parts, gaskets, and structural components developed for scale.

At Bajaj Auto, I led end-to-end design and validation efforts across multiple production programs, working closely with quality, manufacturing, and supplier teams to deliver cost-effective and robust designs. I’m now focused on transitioning into consumer hardware and consumer electronics, while continuing to build hands-on experience in electromechanical integration through research and project-based work.
