## About Me Hi! I’m **Sudarsan Srinivasan**, a passionate Mechanical and Product Design Engineer with over 3.5 years of experience in the automotive industry, currently advancing my expertise through a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington. My core strength lies in combining **mechanical design** (CAD, DFM/DFA, GD&T, FEA) with cross-disciplinary capabilities in **mechatronics, robotics, and automation** — building solutions that are not only robust but also intelligent and future-ready. At Bajaj Auto, I contributed to full-vehicle programs, driving innovations across engine systems, structural components, and cost-saving initiatives.
