About Me

Hi! I’m Sudarsan Srinivasan, a passionate Mechanical and Product Design Engineer with over 3.5 years of experience in the automotive industry, currently advancing my expertise through a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington.

My core strength lies in combining mechanical design (CAD, DFM/DFA, GD&T, FEA) with cross-disciplinary capabilities in mechatronics, robotics, and automation — building solutions that are not only robust but also intelligent and future-ready.

At Bajaj Auto, I contributed to full-vehicle programs, driving innovations across engine systems, structural components, and cost-saving initiatives. Now, I’m expanding into robotics, control systems, and automation design through projects and research that fuse mechanical engineering with cutting-edge technology.

Core Highlights:

  • Mechanical Design: Siemens NX, SolidWorks, CATIA, DFM/DFA, GD&T
  • Simulation & Analysis: ANSYS, HyperWorks, FEA, DFMEA, DVP
  • Robotics & Controls: MATLAB, Simulink, ROS, Python, C++
  • Quality & Optimization: VAVE, cost reduction, design validation, supplier collaboration

I’m eager to shape the future of mechanical products by blending design excellence with automation intelligence. Let’s connect!
